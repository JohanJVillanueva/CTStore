About Us

CaviteTech is a tech enthusiast, content creator, and keyboard reviewer from Cavite, Philippines. He is known for his in-depth keyboard reviews on his YouTube channel, which has over 4.8K subscribers. In addition to his YouTube channel, CaviteTech is also active on Facebook, where he shares keyboard news, reviews, and modding tips. He is also a member of the The Grand Lason R2, a collective of Filipino keyboard content creators. CaviteTech is passionate about keyboards and technology, and he enjoys sharing his knowledge and insights with others. He is a valuable resource for anyone interested in learning more about keyboards and mechanical switches.
